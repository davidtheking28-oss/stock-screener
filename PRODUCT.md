# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Israeli retail trader, fluent in Minervini's SEPA methodology. Scans US equities daily or weekly looking for momentum setups. Uses a desktop browser, typically in the evening or early morning before the market opens. Knows what RS Rating, Trend Template, and EPS growth mean — needs no hand-holding. Wants signal, not noise.

## Product Purpose

A single-page stock screener that filters the entire US equity universe to a short list of actionable candidates using Minervini's Trend Template (8 conditions) combined with fundamental quality gates (EPS growth, revenue growth, ROE). The tool replaces a manual TradingView scan and lets the trader focus on reviewing finalists, not hunting for setups.

## Brand Personality

Precise, decisive, institutional-grade. Like a Bloomberg terminal built for one trader who has strong opinions. Three words: sharp, trustworthy, focused.

## Anti-references

- Robinhood: gamified, pastel, fun-first. This tool is not a toy.
- Generic crypto dashboards: neon overload, dark-for-dark's-sake. No glowing grid lines.
- TradingView's own screener: cluttered, feature-bloated, designed by committee.
- Every "AI trading dashboard" Dribbble shot: glassmorphism cards, gradient text, hero metrics.

## Design Principles

1. **Signal over chrome.** Every visual element either communicates data or creates hierarchy. Decoration that doesn't earn its place is removed.
2. **Respect the expert.** The user knows the domain. Labels are short. Tooltips exist but aren't forced. No onboarding modals.
3. **Trust through precision.** Numbers are formatted consistently. Alignment is tight. A misaligned column breaks trust more than a missing feature.
4. **Dark because it's correct.** The user scans this at a desk, often in low light, alongside TradingView and broker windows. Dark background reduces eye strain and keeps focus on the numbers.
5. **Speed is a feature.** The screener should feel instant to interact with. No gratuitous animations, no layout shifts, no loading spinners that outlast their usefulness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Color is never the only indicator (green/red bars have numeric labels). RTL Hebrew layout throughout.
